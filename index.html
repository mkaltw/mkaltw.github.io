Go ahead and take a look at alwaysownyourplatform.com.

The Internet can be a beautiful place. A decentralized web offers the diversity our modern society tries to embrace, yet major tech corporations dominate our communications. The centralized platforms they operate can offer a lot but they are also dangerous. They can easily be used to monitor our behaviour and censor free speech. I believe this is not how the web -- and our society -- should work.

Privacy

Learn to protect yourself from online spying. The EFF provides a guide about Surveillance Self-Defense. Start from there.

Check out privacytools.io too for some more tips about software you might want to use.

Use the Tor Browser if you want to have an extra layer of anonymity online. Or just use it because you can.

If you want to learn more about how Tor works, check out Computerphile's playlist. They explain it very well and there is a lot more great content on their channel.
